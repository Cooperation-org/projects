# Roles

## Champion

Brought the project in. Accountable for client relationship and project success.

**Responsibilities:**
- Landed the work, owns the client relationship
- Authority to give warnings and remove/replace people (including Lead)
- Ensures deliverables meet client expectations
- May be separate from Lead

**Skills:** [relations](skills/relations/) | [communication](skills/base/communication.md)

---

## Lead

Serves the team and worries about everything. Creates clarity.

**Responsibilities:**
- Standup is heartbeat - hold regular standups
- Create clarity for team, ask questions of stakeholders
- Ensure milestones hit, raise flags early
- Each member has a clear next step
- Use [project template](templates/MAIN.md) - keep taiga/project docs/folders current
- Immediate response to stakeholders (same business day, ideally <1hr)
- Always have a next deadline in mind

**Skills:** [leading](skills/leading/) | [clarity](skills/base/clarity.md)

---

## Developer (Team Member)

Ships working code daily. Follows patterns.

**Responsibilities:**
- Best practices for PRs, code review, design patterns
- Small quick PRs, ping for review
- Every repo has README with local dev & deployment steps
- Ask for priorities, raise flags, be noisy if blocked
- Warn if deadline at risk
- Check for breadcrumbs, leave them

**Skills:** [engineering](skills/engineering/) | [How to Jump on a Moving Project](https://www.whatscookin.us/how-to-jump-on-a-moving-project/)

---

## Marketing (Team Member)

Amplifies the work. Tells the story.

**Responsibilities:**
- Keep up on projects, ask leads/devs to share accomplishments
- Draft pieces ahead, share for internal review
- Align with core goals, add pings to allies
- Develop relationships with journalists, influencers, allies
- Track business development strategies

**Skills:** [marketing](skills/marketing/)

---

## Mentor / Connector

Gives time and expertise. Sets boundaries.

**Responsibilities:**
- Share availability and skillset in #mentors channel and member spreadsheet
- Give feedback in real time when available
- Set boundaries - help but don't take project responsibility (in this role)

**Skills:** [relations](skills/relations/) | [communication](skills/base/communication.md)

---

## Volunteer / Intern

Learns by doing. Earns trust through work.

**Responsibilities:**
- Report in #standup: what you did, what you'll do, blockers
- If you promise something, do it on time OR communicate early
- Focus on outcomes - what are we achieving in the external world?
- Make runbooks, document steps
- LESS is more - few clean organized words/code beat many messy ones
- Take care of yourself, don't burn out

**Onboarding:**
1. Read [Playing on an Organic Team](https://www.whatscookin.us/playing-on-an-organic-team/)
2. Read [How to Jump on a Moving Project](https://www.whatscookin.us/how-to-jump-on-a-moving-project/)
3. Complete onboarding tasks (see [onboarding/](onboarding/README.md))
4. Get added to #standup, taiga, and relevant project channels

**Skills:** [learning](skills/learning/) | [base](skills/base/)
